Shipping Container Art

Industrial shipping containers have become the canvas for artists around the world. Creative's have transformed shipping containers, in colorful and richly detailed illustrations, portraits and typographic treatments, reinterpreting the shipping structures as architectural surfaces for artistry and imagination.
